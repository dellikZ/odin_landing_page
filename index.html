About Pet Express

Pet Express is a non-profit organization from Bucharest, Romania which saves pets and has the mission to find them a household full of protection and love. We started this journey quite recenlty, but we are working hard to provide all the animals we rescue with everything they need before they are adopted.

The steps

You will send an adoption application to our email and write a little about why you want to adopt a pet.
If you are accepted, there will be an online interview with our team.
A member of our team will come to your house to see if it is pet friendly.
We sign the papers and the you cant start your brand new life with your beloved furball.
